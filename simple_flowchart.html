📈 Summary of Results

Performance Summary:
• Static Baseline: 0.202 Recall@20 (ideal conditions)
• Dynamic Baseline: 0.173 Recall@20 (-14.3% drop - problem confirmed)
• Dynamic Solution: 0.176 Recall@20 (-12.9% drop - improvement achieved)
• Burst Pattern: +2.1% improvement (unexpected resilience)
• Shift Pattern: +17.5% improvement (major discovery)

Key Insight: DCCF shows three distinct behaviors:
1. Vulnerable to gradual dynamic changes (needs our solution)
2. Resilient to sudden burst patterns (naturally robust)
3. Benefits from focus shift patterns (unexpected advantage)
